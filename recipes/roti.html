Ingredients

  • Wheat Flour
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Stir flour, water, oil and salt together
  2. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  3. Divide dough into equal pieces
  4. Heat a skillet and cook one roti at a time. until dark spots appear
  5. Cover with foil and bake
